Refund Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026  ·  Terms of Service  ·  Privacy Policy  ·  Contact Us

This Refund Policy explains when refunds are available for purchases made on Cosmic Blueprint ("we," "us," "the Service").

1. Nature of the Product

The full report ("Unlock Your Full Blueprint") is a digital product delivered immediately upon successful payment. Because it is accessible right away, purchases are generally treated as final once the report has been viewed.

2. When a Refund Is Available

We will issue a refund if:

  • You were charged but never received access to your report due to a technical error on our end
  • You were charged more than once for the same report
  • You contact us within 7 days of purchase and have not been able to access the content you paid for

3. When a Refund Is Not Available

Refunds are generally not available simply because you changed your mind, or because the report's content did not match your expectations, once it has been successfully delivered and viewed. This reflects the nature of an instantly-delivered digital product rather than any judgment about your individual experience.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email support@getcosmicblueprint.com with the email address used at checkout and a brief description of the issue. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

5. Payment Processor

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider acting as merchant of record. In some cases, approved refunds may be processed directly by that provider; processing times can vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: support@getcosmicblueprint.com
